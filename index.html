About STATCON

STATCON is a leading provider of Statistical consulting and data analysis solutions. With our extensive expertise, we assist individuals and businesses of all sizes in tackling statistical and research problems, including study preparation, data management, analysis, and result interpretation.

Our team of highly skilled statisticians and consultants holds Master's and PhD degrees from reputable universities worldwide. We bring a deep understanding of statistics and research methodologies, ensuring that you receive expert guidance and insights for your projects.

We are committed to delivering comprehensive and tailored solutions that meet your specific needs. Whether it's designing experiments and surveys, establishing hypotheses, providing methodological instructions, or offering interpretation of results, our consultants have the technical knowledge to address your requirements.

At STATCON, we pride ourselves on our friendly support, quick turnaround times, and effective communication. Over 90% of our clients request our assistance more than once, demonstrating their satisfaction with our solutions.

Our expertise extends to various statistical software packages, Such as SPSS, SAS, Stata, R, PYTHON, and more. We employ the latest tools and techniques to ensure accurate data analysis, effective data management, and insightful reporting.

Partnering with STATCON means gaining access to powerful analytics, data-driven solutions, proprietary research, and deep sector insights. We are dedicated to helping you grow, thrive, and adopt best practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have compiled a list of commonly asked questions to help address any inquiries you may have.

  • What services do you offer?

    We offer a range of statistical consulting and data management services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our services include statistical analysis, data cleaning and preparation, research design, survey development, and more.

  • How do I request a consultation?

    To request a consultation, please visit our website and fill out the consultation request form. Provide us with details about your project and requirements, and we will get back to you to schedule a meeting.

  • What are your pricing options?

    Our pricing options vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project. We offer flexible pricing plans and can provide you with a customized quote based on your specific requirements. Contact us to discuss your project and get a detailed pricing estimate.

  • How long does a typical project take?

    The duration of a project depends on various factors, such as the size of the dataset, complexity of the analysis, and specific requirements. We work closely with our clients to establish realistic timelines and ensure timely project completion.

  • Can I request revisions or changes to the analysis?

    Yes, we strive to meet our clients' expectations and ensure their satisfaction. If you require revisions or changes to the analysis, please communicate your feedback to our team, and we will work with you to address your needs.
